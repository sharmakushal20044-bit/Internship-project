Cybersecurity Awareness 🔐

Stay Safe Online ⚠️

🔑 Strong Passwords

Always use strong and unique passwords. A good password includes uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols. Avoid using names or birthdates.

📧 Phishing Attacks

Do not click on unknown email links. Hackers often send fake emails pretending to be banks or companies to steal your information.

🔒 Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA for extra security. Even if someone gets your password, they cannot access your account without the second verification step.

📶 Secure Networks

Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions. Always use trusted and password-protected networks.

💻 Software Updates

Keep your apps and system updated. Updates fix security bugs and protect against new threats.
